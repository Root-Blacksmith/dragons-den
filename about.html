About Us

A shop with a heartbeat

Dragon’s Den is a tabletop game shop in Upper Lake. Cards, dice, miniatures, and more than any of that, the tables to play them on.

Why we’re here

[Tell the real story here.] The honest version, not corporate filler. Who started Dragon’s Den, and the night the idea wouldn’t let go. A back table, a borrowed rulebook, a town that didn’t have a place like this yet.

We opened the Den so this corner of Lake County would have a room where the game comes first and everything happens face to face, around a table, not a screen.

Who runs it

[Names and a few honest lines.] The people behind the counter, what they love to play, and why they’ll happily talk your ear off about it. The card shark, the painter, the forever-GM.

Ask us anything. We’d rather teach you a game than sell you one you won’t open.

There’s a chair for you

Whether you’ve rolled dice for thirty years or never once in your life, you belong at one of our tables. The scariest part of walking into a game shop is feeling like everyone already knows each other. Here, somebody will wave you over. That’s the whole point of the place.
